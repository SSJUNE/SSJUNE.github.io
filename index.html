Biography

Biography

A young but experienced artist who’s never been one to shy away from hard work in pursuit of his passion. Born in Cameroon to two parents who would early on his life decide moving to the USA would be in their family’s best interest. This initial change of environment would be the starting point on the path that would mold his unique perspective on life that would later become the subject of much of his music. Surprisingly enough rap music and this young artist never truly meshed until his teen years when he went from poorly rapping along to some of his favorite artists to writing his own raps at the age of fourteen.

His goal now, is simple, to prove he was right to bet on himself when very few else would.

Music would only continue to have an increasing importance in SSJUNE’s life gradually overshadowing all his other passions like Basketball and Mixed Martial Arts. Now, nearly a decade later SSJUNE has gone from a misunderstood kid who found solace in etching his aspirations and experiences in his beatdown notebook to a nearly unrecognizable entertainer in comparison to his early hay days.
